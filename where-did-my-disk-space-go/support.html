Support

Help for Where Did My Disk Space Go?, a local macOS utility for reviewing disk usage and moving selected items to Trash.

Before cleanup

  • Use Reveal in Finder to inspect files or folders before moving them to Trash.
  • Do not remove files you do not recognize unless you understand what they are for.
  • Items moved to Trash remain recoverable until you empty the Trash.

Folder access

If a scan does not show expected folders, open System Settings, review Privacy & Security settings for Files and Folders, and confirm the app has access to the folders you want it to scan.

Contact

For App Store review, customer support, or privacy questions, contact the developer account owner through the App Store listing. If you are preparing the first submission, use this page as the Support URL and the Privacy Policy page as the Privacy Policy URL in App Store Connect.
